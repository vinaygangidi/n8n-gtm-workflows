# GTM ICP & Segmentation Intelligence Workflow

## Strategic Overview

This workflow provides a data-driven mechanism to continuously refine Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) definitions using real revenue outcomes instead of static assumptions.

It enables GTM leadership to make targeting, segmentation, and resource allocation decisions grounded in empirical deal performance data.

---

## Business Problem Statement

Many organizations define ICP using intuition, legacy assumptions, or anecdotal feedback rather than measurable deal success patterns.

This creates strategic inefficiencies such as:

- Misaligned marketing targeting
- Low lead-to-opportunity conversion rates
- Long sales cycles
- High acquisition cost
- Poor revenue predictability
- Sales team effort spent on low-fit accounts

Without continuous ICP refinement, GTM strategies drift away from actual market reality.

---

## Current Manual Challenges

### 1. Spreadsheet-Driven Analysis

RevOps teams typically:
- Export deal data
- Build pivot tables
- Manually calculate averages
- Attempt to identify patterns

This is slow and rarely repeated consistently.

### 2. Lack of Multi-Dimensional Insights

Manual analysis struggles to combine:
- Deal size
- Sales cycle duration
- source quality
- segment performance
- pipeline behavior

Insights remain shallow.

### 3. Limited Strategic Adoption

Even when insights exist:
- They are not operationalized
- Lead scoring models remain outdated
- Target segments remain broad
- SDR prioritization does not change

---

## AI Agentic Automation Approach

This workflow introduces an automated revenue intelligence loop:

1. Extract closed-won deal data from CRM
2. Enrich with performance metrics
3. Provide structured dataset to AI reasoning engine
4. Generate prioritized ICP attributes
5. Identify anti-ICP signals
6. Recommend scoring weights and GTM actions
7. Distribute insights automatically

The workflow becomes a **continuous GTM strategy calibration mechanism.**

---

## Workflow Architecture

### Data Collection Layer
- CRM deal extraction
- Sales cycle calculation
- ACV normalization

### Intelligence Layer
- Pattern clustering via AI
- Segment frequency analysis
- Revenue impact correlation

### Decision Layer
- ICP prioritization
- Lead scoring recommendations
- Targeting guidance

### Distribution Layer
- Slack insight delivery
- Executive email reports
- Optional downstream scoring automation
