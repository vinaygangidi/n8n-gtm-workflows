# GTM Account 360 Copilot Workflow

## Strategic Overview

Account 360 Copilot is an AI-agentic GTM workflow designed to transform fragmented account research into a structured, repeatable, CRM-anchored execution process.

Modern GTM teams operate across multiple systems such as CRM, product analytics, marketing platforms, and internal communication tools. However, at the moment of action, account context is often incomplete, outdated, or manually assembled.

This workflow introduces an AI orchestration layer that converts raw user input into validated CRM intelligence and executable next steps.

---

## Business Problem Statement

GTM teams lack a reliable, real-time mechanism to obtain a unified account view before engaging prospects or customers.

Key strategic challenges include:

- Account context spread across multiple tools
- Inconsistent lifecycle stage understanding
- Limited visibility into account ownership and engagement status
- Manual research effort before outreach
- Delayed or missed follow-up actions
- Poor prioritization of high-value accounts

As a result, revenue teams often operate reactively instead of proactively.

---

## Current Manual Challenges

### 1. Manual CRM Navigation

Sales representatives must:
- Search CRM records manually
- Validate domain and account identity
- Check lifecycle stage and owner
- Review notes and activity history

This process is time-consuming and error-prone.

### 2. Fragmented Decision Making

Account insights are often:
- Based on incomplete data
- Influenced by personal interpretation
- Not standardized across teams

This leads to inconsistent outreach quality.

### 3. Lack of Structured Action Creation

Even when insights are identified:
- Tasks are not always created
- Follow-ups are forgotten
- Team visibility is limited

Revenue leakage occurs due to execution gaps.

---

## AI Agentic Automation Approach

This workflow introduces an autonomous reasoning layer that:

1. Accepts flexible human input (company name or domain)
2. Resolves authoritative account identity using CRM
3. Normalizes and structures account intelligence
4. Uses AI to generate contextual understanding
5. Converts insights into CRM tasks
6. Broadcasts recommendations to GTM teams

The workflow functions as a **stateless decision engine** ensuring each execution produces a durable business outcome.

---

## Workflow Architecture

### Input Layer
- Chat trigger / webhook
- Domain extraction and normalization

### Data Resolution Layer
- Batch CRM search
- Intelligent company matching logic
- Authoritative CRM lookup

### AI Reasoning Layer
- Contextual account summarization
- Risk and opportunity identification
- Next-best-action generation

### Execution Layer
- HubSpot task creation
- Slack notification
- Structured JSON output for downstream automation
